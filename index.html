Why do I need a website for my business?

A website helps you promote your business online and establish credibility with your customers. For customers to find you online, it’s important to have a professional website that offers helpful, relevant content and provides a good experience for users.

Should I use a website builder or hire a website designer?

We offer both options. If you need a website quickly, then we have an easy-to-use website builder with over a hundred templates to choose from. Once you find a template you love, you can customize it to fit your needs and get online fast. If you’re short on time, then let our experts design a custom website that checks all the boxes. You’ll get a professional design with fresh content written for your customers and search engines in mind.

What is a domain name and why do I need one?

A domain name is the URL of your website. Think of it as the address for your customers to find your website online. A branded, keyword-rich domain name is a great way to give your business a more professional presence online, build credibility, increase brand awareness and improve your search engine rankings.

Why do businesses use Web.com for their digital marketing?

We provide a full range of online marketing services for every type and size of business and budget, with campaigns designed for both local and global markets. Our Pay-Per-Click (PPC) agents are specially trained to assist you with Google advertising, a service which allows you to choose specific keywords and appear in ads at the top of search pages. We also offer search engine optimization (SEO) services to determine the right keywords and content strategy for raising your search ranking. If you’d like to connect with more people on social media, we’ll help you grow your audience in the places your customers spend time online.

How can I keep my website secure online?

You can keep your website secure and your customers’ information safe with an SSL certificate. An SSL certificate will validate your identity online and encrypt private information, such as payment and customer account information. A secure website will gain your customers’ trust and improve your search engine performance
